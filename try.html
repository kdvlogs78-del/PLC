Our Journey

Our Story

Suryatanaya Innovation LLP was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional industrial processes and modern automation technology. We recognized that many industries were struggling with outdated systems, inefficient processes, and the challenges of integrating Industry 4.0 technologies.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make advanced industrial automation accessible, reliable, and effective for businesses of all sizes. From our base in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we've grown into a trusted partner for industries seeking to optimize their operations through smart automation solutions.

Today, we specialize in comprehensive automation services including PLC programming, SCADA integration, instrumentation, industrial networking, and IoT solutions. Our expertise spans across process industries, manufacturing plants, water treatment facilities, and beyond.
